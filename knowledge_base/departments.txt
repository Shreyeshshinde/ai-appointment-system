Cardiology:
  - Treats heart-related symptoms such as chest pain, palpitations, and shortness of breath.
  - Common procedures include ECG, echocardiogram, angiography.

Neurology:
  - Deals with brain and nervous system issues like headaches, seizures, dizziness, stroke.

Dermatology:
  - Treats skin-related issues such as rashes, acne, eczema, fungal infections.

Orthopedics:
  - Handles bone, joint, and muscle problems such as fractures, arthritis, back pain.

Pulmonology:
  - Manages lung and respiratory problems like asthma, cough, wheezing, and shortness of breath.

General Medicine:
  - First-level diagnosis for fever, fatigue, infections, and general body discomfort.
